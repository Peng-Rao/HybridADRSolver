Detailed Description

template<int dim>
struct HybridADRSolver::ScratchData< dim >

Scratch data for the WorkStream assembly (Optimized).

This struct holds thread-local data required for assembly, including pre-allocated caching arrays for shape values, gradients, and coefficients to minimize redundant computations in the inner assembly loops.
