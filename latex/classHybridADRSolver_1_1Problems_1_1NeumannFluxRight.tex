class Hybrid\+ADRSolver\+::\+Problems\+::\+Neumann\+Flux\+Right$<$ dim $>$}
Analytic Neumann flux for the Right boundary (x=1). 

Calculates $ \nabla u \cdot \mathbf{n} $ at x=1, where  $ \mathbf{n} =
(1, 0, \dots)^T $. Since $ \cos(\pi) = -1 $, the flux is negative.
